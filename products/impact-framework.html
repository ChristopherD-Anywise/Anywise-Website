What is Impact Measurement & Reporting?

Government programs, research initiatives, and community organizations need to demonstrate impact — but data lives in 50 different spreadsheets across 30 different teams, and nobody has time to chase it all down.

We design frameworks that standardize how teams collect data, then automatically pull it together into dashboards that show what's working and what isn't. No more manual consolidation. No more quarterly reporting panic.

One federal program we worked with went from 60% of teams submitting reports to 95%, and cut their reporting cycle from weeks to days. They spent less time on paperwork and more time actually running the program.
