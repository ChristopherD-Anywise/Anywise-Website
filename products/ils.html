Overview

What is Integrated Logistics Support?

Defence and industry need more than equipment — they need maintenance plans, spare parts strategies, training documentation, and support contracts that work in the real world. We develop ILS frameworks and technical publications that keep capabilities operational long after initial delivery.

We don't just write manuals. We embed with project teams to understand how the system actually gets maintained, where the supply chain breaks down, and what maintainers need to know. Then we produce the support concepts, operational manuals, and sustainment plans that Defence evaluators actually want to see.

For both new and existing technical publications, we use WISDOM to accelerate the entire process. Creating new documentation is 50% faster. Editing and updating existing manuals takes 80% less time. For legacy publications, WISDOM transforms thousands of pages of PDFs into searchable, navigable knowledge models where engineers can ask questions in plain language and get answers pulled from across the entire technical library.
