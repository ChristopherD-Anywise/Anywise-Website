Overview

What is AIDE?

AIDE is the enterprise intelligence platform that sits above Anywise's domain-specific tools (WISDOM, ENG|AIDE, FABHUMS, and CAMP|AIDE) and integrates their outputs into a single operational picture. Where the domain platforms answer specific questions, AIDE answers the enterprise question: given everything we know across all domains, where should we focus our attention and resources right now?

Designed for senior leaders and enterprise operations teams, AIDE provides the cross-domain correlation, pattern recognition, and priority synthesis that are impossible when domain information exists only in separate systems.

Why AIDE

Key Benefits

Unified Operational Picture

Single pane of glass across all operational domains. No more switching between systems or manually correlating data from separate platforms.

Cross-Domain Intelligence

Identify relationships between events in different domains that individual platform operators would never see. Strategic insight from operational data.

Executive Decision Support

Outputs calibrated for senior leader decision-making: priority-ordered, evidence-grounded, and formatted for rapid assimilation.

Continuous Situational Awareness

Automated monitoring across all connected platforms with intelligent alerting that surfaces only what genuinely requires leadership attention.

Process

How It Works

  1. 01

    Connect & Integrate

    AIDE connects to all Anywise domain platforms and, through its API layer, to existing enterprise systems including ERP, HR, finance, and C2 platforms, creating a unified data foundation.

  2. 02

    Correlate & Synthesise

    Cross-domain analytical reasoning identifies patterns, dependencies, and emerging risks that span operational boundaries. AIDE surfaces the connections that siloed systems cannot see.

  3. 03

    Present & Prioritise

    Outputs are calibrated for each user role, from operational staff to CXOs, with appropriate depth, framing, and priority ordering for each audience.
