Overview

What is CAMP|AIDE?

CAMP|AIDE is a facilities intelligence platform that addresses the compounding complexity of managing large, distributed estates where operational readiness depends on infrastructure performance. Defence and government facilities planners face a unique challenge: facilities are simultaneously operational assets, safety environments, and long-lived capital investments requiring lifecycle stewardship.

CAMP|AIDE integrates the data streams relevant to each of these dimensions and provides planning teams with an integrated view of their estate — enabling better decisions about investment, maintenance, and operational risk.

Why CAMP|AIDE

Key Benefits

Process

How It Works

  1. 01

    Map & Model

    CAMP|AIDE creates a digital model of your estate from existing asset registers, drawings, and condition records — establishing the baseline for ongoing management.

  2. 02

    Monitor & Alert

    Continuous monitoring of facility condition, compliance status, and operational readiness flags emerging issues and compliance deadlines before they become failures or penalties.

  3. 03

    Plan & Optimise

    Investment planning tools model the impact of different maintenance and upgrade scenarios on operational readiness and compliance, supporting better capital allocation decisions.
