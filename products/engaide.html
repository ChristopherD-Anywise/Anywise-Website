Overview

What is ENG|AIDE?

ENG|AIDE is an engineering intelligence platform purpose-built for complex, high-consequence operational environments. It continuously monitors system health, flags emerging risks, and provides engineers with actionable recommendations to prevent failures before they occur.

Designed for defence and industrial environments where engineering failure is not an option, ENG|AIDE replaces reactive maintenance with predictive operational awareness. It integrates with existing engineering management systems and delivers outputs in formats that operational engineers actually use.

Process

How It Works

  1. 01

    Monitor & Detect

    ENG|AIDE integrates with SCADA, maintenance management, and sensor systems to create a continuous monitoring layer across your asset base.

  2. 02

    Analyse & Correlate

    Pattern recognition and interdependency analysis identifies emerging risks and correlates signals across systems to detect complex failure precursors.

  3. 03

    Recommend & Report

    Prioritised maintenance recommendations are delivered with supporting evidence, risk scores, and projected outcomes for each course of action.
