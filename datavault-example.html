Our purpose

Built on principles
that don't compromise.

Security — End-to-end encryption and zero-knowledge architecture ensure your data remains yours alone. We never have access to your keys.
Transparency — We believe in open, honest communication about how data flows through our systems. Audit logs, open-source SDKs, no hidden data practices.

Security by design,
not by default.
