🟡 Accessibility (WCAG AA)
A1

Structural landmarks — <main> + skip link

No <main> element. No skip-to-content link. Screen readers and keyboard users can't navigate efficiently.

A2

Alt text — images and decorative icons

Blog thumbnails have empty alt="". Product card icons and decoration need aria-hidden or descriptive alt.

A3

Colour contrast — secondary text

--text-secondary (#9a9d95) on dark background may fail WCAG AA (4.5:1 ratio required for normal text).

A4

Mobile nav — focus trap + z-index fix

Menu opens but focus isn't moved into it. z-index 999 is below the nav bar (1000) causing layering issues.

Clicking the Anywise wordmark goes nowhere (href="#"). Should link to home.
