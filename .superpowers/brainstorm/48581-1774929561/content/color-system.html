Borders, Accents & Shadows

Dark Mode

  • --border: rgba(255,255,255,0.07)
  • --border-accent: rgba(57,168,73,0.22)
  • --accent: #39a849
  • --accent-dim: #2d7f36
  • Shadows: black-based, high opacity

Light Mode (Proposed)

  • --border: rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
  • --border-accent: rgba(57,168,73,0.25)
  • --accent: #2d7f36 — slightly darker for contrast on white
  • --accent-dim: #39a849
  • Shadows: green-tinted, lower opacity, softer spread

Note: All light-mode background colors are tinted slightly toward green to maintain brand cohesion. No pure whites (#fff) for page backgrounds — only for elevated surfaces like cards. The accent green darkens slightly (#2d7f36) to maintain WCAG AA contrast on light backgrounds.
