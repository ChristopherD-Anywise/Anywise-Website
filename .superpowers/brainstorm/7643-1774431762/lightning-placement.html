Lightning + Earth Composition

Where do the energy beams sit relative to the earth? All options use a realistic dark earth with city lights and green atmospheric edge glow.

A: Energy Descending Into Earth

Lightning beams stream down from above, converging on the earth. Suggests power flowing into the planet. Earth sits in lower half, hero text content occupies the upper space alongside the beams.

B: Earth Right, Lightning Left

Large earth anchored right (partially cropped), subtle lightning beams on the left behind the text content area. Asymmetric, editorial composition. Text has clean space, earth provides gravitas on the side.

C: Earth as Full Backdrop

Massive earth fills the hero, slightly dimmed. Lightning beams pass through/across it. Text overlays directly on top. Immersive and cinematic — the earth IS the background, not a floating element in it.

Reminder: The earth will be a realistic dark satellite image with visible city lights. The green atmospheric rim glow matches brand colors. Lightning beams will be real-time animated GLSL shaders — organic, flickering, alive.
