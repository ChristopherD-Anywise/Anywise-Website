Hero Background Direction

Which visual approach for the WebGL hero feels right for Anywise's defence/government identity?

A: Lightning Energy Beam + Dark Planet

Central vertical energy beam with GLSL lightning shaders in green hues. A dark planet sphere sits in the lower half. Moody, powerful, defence-grade. Closest to the Hero Odyssey reference.

B: Atmospheric Planet with Orbital Rings

Centered glowing planet with orbital ring paths around it. Subtle green atmospheric glow. More sci-fi/space, less raw energy. Clean and elegant.

C: Full Hybrid — Lightning + Planet + Particles

Combines it all: GLSL lightning beams converging on a dark planet, floating green particles scattered around. Maximum visual impact. The "go big" option — layered energy, atmosphere, and depth.

Note: These are static mockups showing the visual direction. The actual implementation will use real-time WebGL shaders for the lightning (animated, flickering, organic) and CSS/canvas for the planet with atmospheric glow effects. All in Anywise brand greens.
