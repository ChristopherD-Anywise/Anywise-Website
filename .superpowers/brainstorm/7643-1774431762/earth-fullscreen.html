What Changed From Previous Design

Before (old spec)
  • Earth was a ~500px floating sphere
  • Positioned at 55% from top
  • Lightning behind, earth in front
  • Parallax scroll at 0.3x rate
Now (updated)
  • Earth fills entire viewport as background
  • Full-bleed, like current Unsplash image
  • Lightning overlays on top of earth
  • Scroll-driven zoom: scale 1.0 -> ~2.5x
  • Content fades out, earth zooms, next section emerges

Implementation is simpler now: The earth is just a full-bleed background image (like the current design) with CSS transform:scale() driven by scroll position. No CSS sphere needed. The lightning WebGL canvas overlays on top. As you scroll, the earth scales up (zooming in), hero text fades out, and the dark gradient transitions you into the capabilities section below.
