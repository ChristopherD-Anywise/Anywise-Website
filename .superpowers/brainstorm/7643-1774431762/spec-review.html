Key Design Decisions

Lightning Effect

Single vertical energy curtain with FBM noise distortion. Medium intensity (0.5), brand green hue (130). 10 octaves desktop, 6 on mobile.

Earth Sphere

CSS-only with NASA nighttime texture. Slow 60s rotation (background-position shift). Green atmospheric rim glow. Positioned at 55% from top.

Scroll Behavior

Canvas + earth parallax at 0.3x scroll rate. Canvas opacity fades with content via --hero-fade. Rendering pauses when off-screen.

Accessibility

prefers-reduced-motion pauses WebGL and earth rotation. Both elements aria-hidden. WebGL fallback to CSS gradient if unsupported.
